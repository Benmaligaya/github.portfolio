COS News Of this Quarter

COS Week Goes in Full Swing!

On February 24, 2025, COS Week is currently at its full peak, filled with fun and high spirits across the College of Science! Many activities are in progress: sports and physical events are being held in the URSM Gymnasium and Quadrangle, while indoor activities such as E-Sports competitions take place in the GMD Room and the new COS building. Students from all departments compete in various events, showcasing skill, camaraderie, and COS pride in this much-anticipated annual celebration.

NSTP Project in High Praise!

CONGRATULATIONS to our College of Science NSTP Team led by Dr. Ricardo R. Ariston for being a 1st Place for the Cleanliness and Campus Beautification Contest held on April 24, 2025 at Function Hall, University of Rizal System Morong Campus, Morong, Rizal. All the efforts and commitment have made a significant impact in creating a cleaner, greener, and more beautiful environment for all.
