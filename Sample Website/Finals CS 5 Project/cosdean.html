The Dean of College of Science

Dr. Wilma R. Lomangaya

About the Dean: Dr. Wilma R. Lomangaya is the current Dean of the College of Science
at the University of Rizal System (URS) Morong Campus. She was appointed to this position
in August 2024, succeeding Dr. Jose E. Binaluyo Jr. In her role, Dr. Lomangaya leads the
College of Science, which offers programs such as Bachelor of Science in Biology, Mathematics,
Psychology, Human services and Computer Science.
