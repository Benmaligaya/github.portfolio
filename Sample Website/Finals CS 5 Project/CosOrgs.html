Organizations

College of Science Student Body (COSSB)

COSSB is the highest governing organization in the college of science/Mother organization of the college of science

Technology Innovators through Programming and Algorithm Sciences (TIPA)

Tipa is the official organization for the Computer science course, and is in charge of holding events and taking charge regarding Computer science

College of Science Athletic Association (COSAA)

The Official athletic and sports organization of the college of science, it is responsible for any sports related event/activity regarding the college of science

Math Circle

The official BS Mathematics Organization responisble for any events and collaborations regarding BS Math

Biology Student Association (BIOSA)

The Official and main Organization of BS Biology, and is responisble for any events or activites regarding BS Biology

Samahan ng mga Mag-aaral sa Sikolohiya (SMS)

An organization that promotes psychological awareness with an embodied interest and ideals, it is the main and official Organization of BS Psychology

The Essence

The Official Publication and main journalism organizational branch of the college of science

Students and Advocates Responsive to Gender and Development

The Main Organization of the College of science regarding advocation of gender related events and programs, gender development and student gender identity

Student Researcher Organization (SRO)

This is the official Organization of the URSM College of Science's Student Researchers

Baklurang Gabay Patnubay (BGP)

the main student body of the BS Human services
